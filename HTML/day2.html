Day 2

Day two of BitsCon IT Congress 2024 commenced with a series of in-depth panel discussions, addressing pressing issues at the intersection of technology and society. Ethical considerations in artificial intelligence, data privacy regulations, and the ethical use of emerging technologies emerged as central themes, prompting introspection among industry stakeholders. Meanwhile, startup pitch sessions provided a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to showcase their disruptive ideas and seek investment opportunities. Venture capitalists and angel investors avidly scoped out the next big innovation, fueling excitement and anticipation within the startup ecosystem. In parallel sessions, academic researchers presented their latest findings in fields such as machine learning, cybersecurity, and human-computer interaction. The cross-pollination of ideas between academia and industry spurred interdisciplinary collaborations and paved the way for groundbreaking research initiatives. Throughout the day, attendees engaged in thought-provoking conversations during networking breaks and social events, forging lasting connections and fostering a sense of community within the global tech ecosystem.
